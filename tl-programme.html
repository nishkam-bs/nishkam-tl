All teaching staff

Programme Overview

Each session includes a CPD presentation, facilitator-led activities, self-directed reflection, and an implementation plan.

01
Behaviour for Learning
Establishing calm, purposeful classrooms through consistent routines, clear expectations, and strong lesson starts and endings. Introduces the Four Pillars of BFL — Environment, Routine, Relationship, and Consistency.
Secure calm and purposeful learning environments
Embed consistent routines for entry, instruction, and transitions
Ensure first-time compliance and strong lesson starts and endings
RoutinesEntry & ExitComplianceFour Pillars
CPD Presentation
02
Explaining & Modelling
Developing clarity in teaching by making thinking visible. Unpacks the I Do, We Do, You Do framework — modelling thinking step by step so pupils move confidently toward independent practice.
Use I Do, We Do, You Do to structure learning
Model thinking step by step with precision
Support pupils toward independent practice
I Do We Do You DoModellingClarity

03
Checking for Understanding
Using real-time assessment to identify learning and adapt teaching responsively. Mini-whiteboards, hinge questions, monitoring hesitation, and explicit vocabulary teaching.
Use mini-whiteboards and quick checks to assess all pupils
Monitor hesitation and misconceptions in real time
Teach and revisit key vocabulary explicitly
WhiteboardsAFLVocabularyMisconceptions

04
Questioning
Strengthening classroom dialogue through structured questioning. Cold calling, Think Pair Share — ensuring every pupil is thinking, not just those with hands up.
Ask purposeful questions to the whole class
Use cold calling to promote accountability and inclusion
Structure Think, Pair, Share to deepen understanding
Cold CallingThink Pair ShareInclusion

05
Probing Questions
Extending thinking through follow-up questioning. Clarify, justify, extend — distributing cognitive work across the class and surfacing misconceptions through dialogue.
Use probing questions to clarify, justify, and extend thinking
Distribute thinking across the class
Identify and address misconceptions through dialogue

SEND Support & Adaptive Teaching
Ensuring inclusive classrooms through adaptive teaching, effective deployment of additional adults, and interventions that promote pupil independence.
Adapt teaching to meet diverse learning needs
Deploy additional adults effectively
Deliver interventions that promote independence

Embedding & Refining Practice
Consolidating key strategies through structured reflection, coaching conversations, and sharing effective practice across the school.

Share effective strategies and solutions

Review & Impact
