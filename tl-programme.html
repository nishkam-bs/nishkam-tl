T&L Excellence Programme

Embedding consistent, inclusive classroom practice to improve behaviour, learning, and outcomes for all pupils.

All teaching staff (ECTs & experienced)
8 sessions · 60–75 mins each
Interactive CPD with rehearsal
Sessions

Eight sessions. One standard.

Each session includes a CPD presentation, facilitator-led activities, self-directed reflection tasks, and an implementation plan to take away.

01
Behaviour for Learning
Establishing calm, purposeful classrooms through consistent routines, clear expectations, and strong lesson starts and endings. Introduces the Four Pillars of BFL — Environment, Routine, Relationship, and Consistency — with a scripted entry sequence, routine audit, live rehearsal in pairs, scenario-based challenges, and a take-away implementation plan.
Secure calm and purposeful learning environments
Embed consistent routines for entry, instruction, and transitions
Ensure first-time compliance and strong lesson starts and endings
RoutinesEntry & ExitComplianceFour Pillars
📥 CPD Presentation
02
Explaining & Modelling
Developing clarity in teaching by making thinking visible through effective modelling. Unpacks the I Do, We Do, You Do framework and shows how to model thinking step by step with precision — breaking complex processes into small, learnable chunks so pupils move confidently toward independent practice.
Use I Do, We Do, You Do to structure learning
Model thinking step by step with precision
Support pupils to move confidently towards independent practice
I Do We Do You DoModellingClarityIndependence

03
Checking for Understanding
Using assessment strategies to identify learning in real time and adapt teaching responsively. Equips teachers with practical tools — mini-whiteboards, hinge questions, quick checks — and shows how to monitor hesitation, spot misconceptions early, and teach key vocabulary explicitly.
Use mini-whiteboards and quick checks to assess all pupils
Monitor hesitation and misconceptions in real time
Teach and revisit key vocabulary explicitly
WhiteboardsAFLVocabularyMisconceptions

04
Questioning
Strengthening classroom dialogue through structured questioning that ensures full participation. Covers purposeful whole-class questioning, cold calling to promote accountability and inclusion, and structured Think Pair Share — ensuring every pupil is thinking, not just those with hands up.
Ask purposeful questions to the whole class
Use cold calling to promote accountability and inclusion
Structure Think, Pair, Share to deepen understanding
Cold CallingThink Pair ShareInclusionAccountability

05
Probing Questions
Extending thinking through effective probing and follow-up questioning. Teaches how to use probing questions that clarify, justify, and extend thinking — distributing cognitive work across the class and using dialogue as the primary tool for surfacing and addressing misconceptions.
Use probing questions to clarify, justify, and extend thinking
Distribute thinking across the class
Identify and address misconceptions through dialogue
ProbingMisconceptionsDialogueExtend
📥 Coming soon
06
SEND Support & Adaptive Teaching
Ensuring inclusive classrooms through adaptive teaching, effective in-class support, and purposeful interventions. Shows how to adapt teaching to meet diverse needs without lowering expectations, deploy additional adults effectively, and deliver interventions that promote pupil independence rather than dependence.
Adapt teaching to meet diverse learning needs
Deploy additional adults effectively
Deliver high-quality SEND interventions that promote independence
SENDAdaptive TeachingIndependenceInclusion
📥 Coming soon
07
Embedding & Refining Practice
Consolidating key strategies and strengthening consistency through reflection and coaching. Gives teachers structured time to reflect on impact, refine classroom practice based on evidence, and share effective strategies — building a shared professional culture of continuous improvement.
Reflect on impact and refine classroom practice
Strengthen consistency across classrooms
Share effective strategies and solutions
ReflectionCoachingConsistencySharing

08
Review & Impact
Evaluating programme impact and setting clear next steps for sustained improvement. Teachers review progress against agreed teaching standards, identify strengths and priorities for development, and agree concrete next steps to embed and sustain their practice.
Review progress against agreed teaching standards
Identify strengths and priorities for development
Agree next steps to embed and sustain practice
