Ethos

Teaching & Learning at Nishkam School

At Nishkam, the classroom is where virtues are lived, identities are shaped, and every child is truly seen.

Pillar I
Faith-Inspired

Drawing on the wisdom of all traditions. The teacher who approaches their craft with humility and openness creates the conditions for every child to flourish.

Pillar II
Virtues-Led

Peacefulness, diligence and determination — practised in ourselves first. Consistent, caring classrooms are an act of professional virtue.

Pillar III
Aspiring for Excellence

Becoming the best teachers we can possibly be. Rehearsing our craft, seeking honest feedback, and never settling for less than every pupil deserves.

"To empower children with virtues that enable them to excel academically and spiritually, inspiring them to serve humanity selflessly — with an abundance of love, compassion and forgiveness."
Nishkam School Trust · Mission Statement
Virtues & Golden Threads

The threads that run through everything.

The Golden Threads are the spiritual and moral foundations of life at Nishkam School.

Golden Thread 1
love and forgivenessvsenmity and hate
Virtue
love

Love is caring for someone, wanting to be near them and wanting to share with them. It is a special feeling that fills your heart. Love is treating other people just as you would like them to treat you — with care and respect.

I express love for the Creator by caring for others and creation.
Golden Thread 2
peace & collaborationvsblame & conflict
Virtue
peacefulness

Peacefulness is an inner sense of calm. It is facing your fears and then letting them go. Peacefulness is giving up the love of power for the power of love. Peace in the world begins with peace in your heart.

I use peaceful language even when I am angry. I appreciate differences.
Golden Thread 3
Trust in God
Virtue
trust

Trust is having faith, hope and a positive outlook. It is having confidence that the right thing will come about, without trying to control it. We know we are never alone and that God is always with us.

I believe there is some lesson in everything that happens. I let trust take away my fears.
