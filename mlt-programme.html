Programme Overview

Each session includes a CPD presentation, facilitator-led activities, self-directed reflection, and practical frameworks to implement.

What is a Middle Leader?
Exploring the unique role of middle leaders as the bridge between whole-school strategy and classroom practice. Defines the role, identifies the six key traits — credibility, clarity, consistency, communication, courage, coaching — and reflects on how leadership shapes culture.
